What we offer

Built around
your workflow.

A focused hosting service for Discord bots and Express apps — managed entirely through Discord so you never need to leave.

Up to 2 Servers

Host your bots across up to 2 Discord servers. We keep it focused so every project gets proper care.

Express App Hosting

Deploy Express.js apps alongside your bots. Each project gets a public URL — no reverse proxy setup needed.

Kept Online

We monitor your project and manually handle any issues that come up — so it stays running without you having to worry.

Ongoing Support

Need changes, updates, or have issues? Open a ticket anytime and we'll sort it out directly with you.

Secure Env Secrets

Store tokens and API keys as encrypted environment variables. Never expose credentials in your codebase.

100% Discord Native

Everything happens inside your Discord server. No external dashboards, no extra logins.
